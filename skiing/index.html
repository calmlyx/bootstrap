Introduction of DC Products

DC is another trustworthy brand in the snowboarding industry. The main features of the snowboard are strong technology, excellent comfort, as well as a very dazzling pattern matching, which makes it more attractive in the snowfield! In addition to the snowboard, the snowboard boots it produces are also conscientious products of the industry, regardless of quality or style, are very good.

Introduction of Burton Products

Burton, as the largest brand of snowboarding, has been leading the market for many years. Burton brand products from veneers, fixtures to clothing are involved, under which also has R.E.D (protective gear), ANON (snow mirror), GRAVIS (skateboarding shoes), ANOG (clothing). Because BURTON not only has high-tech capabilities, but also combines quite popular elements, so it has been very popular with skaters!

Company official website

Introduction of Flow Products

Plates and shoe fixtures are made, and Antti Autti is a rising star. But the most famous one is their patented fixture technology, which makes the fixture easy to wear, but personal feeling is unsafe. After several jumps, shoes easily fall out of the fixture.

Introduction of K2 Products

Quite a large sports product company, covering skiing, skateboarding, bicycle, mountain climbing and so on. K2 veneer also includes K2, RIDE, 5150. MORROW brands. K2 boards are quite good, but their fixtures also have their own patented technology, similar to FLOW, which can be worn from the back, but different from FLOW, it can also be worn from the front. But the K2 fixator feels like a fast big iron block. Yes, it's too heavy, but it's very strong.
